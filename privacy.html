Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Overview

Burst2GIF is a desktop plugin that runs entirely on your computer. We don't collect, store, or transmit your photos or personal data.

What we collect

  • Email address: Only if you download the free version or make a purchase. Used solely to deliver your download link and important product updates.
  • Payment information: Processed securely by LemonSqueezy. We never see or store your credit card details.

What we don't collect

  • • Your photos (they never leave your computer)
  • • Personal information beyond email

Cookies and tracking

This website uses the following technologies that may collect data:

  • Google Analytics: We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with our website. This service collects information such as your IP address (anonymized), browser type, pages visited, time spent on pages, and referral sources. This data helps us improve our website and marketing. Google Analytics uses cookies to collect this information. You can opt out by declining cookies when prompted or by using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Google may use this information in accordance with their privacy policy: policies.google.com/privacy

Data retention

Your email address is retained for as long as necessary to provide you with product updates and support. If you wish to have your email removed from our records, please contact us and we will delete it within 30 days.

Legal basis for processing (GDPR)

We process your personal data based on the following legal grounds:
